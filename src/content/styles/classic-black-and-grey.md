## The Full Spectrum of Black

Classic Black & Grey tattooing proves that a single color can contain infinite depth. By diluting black ink to various concentrations, artists create smooth gradients, soft shadows, and photorealistic effects that rival any full-color piece. This technique originated in prisons where colored inks weren't available, but it has evolved into one of tattooing's most sophisticated and respected styles.
